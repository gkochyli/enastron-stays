About This Guest Room

A cozy and inviting guest room located in the heart of Skopelos, just 5 minutes from the port, town centre, and beach. Perfect for couples seeking a peaceful getaway on Greece's greenest island.

The room is thoughtfully furnished and equipped with modern comforts. Enjoy the quiet surroundings and beautiful views of the area. A short stroll takes you to the inviting shores, tavernas, and all the charm of Skopelos Town.

This guest room can be combined with our larger Enastron Guesthouse apartment (1 bedroom, sleeps 4) for groups needing more space.
