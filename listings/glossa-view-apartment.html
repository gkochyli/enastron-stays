About This Apartment

Perched high in the tranquil village of Glossa — Skopelos's second-largest settlement at 250 meters above the sea — this charming apartment offers panoramic views of the Aegean that stretch all the way to Skiathos on clear days.

The apartment occupies the upper floor of a traditional Skopelitan house, featuring whitewashed walls, wooden beam ceilings, and a spacious balcony that becomes your private viewing platform for spectacular sunrises and island life below.

Glossa is a peaceful village with authentic tavernas, a bakery, and a genuine local atmosphere untouched by mass tourism. The nearby Loutraki harbor—a quick drive or scenic walk downhill—offers ferry connections and seaside dining on the waterfront.
