About This Villa

Set within the historic heart of Skopelos Town — honored as a Traditional Settlement of Outstanding Beauty — this lovingly restored stone villa is surrounded by centuries-old olive trees and a fragrant garden of jasmine and bougainvillea.

The villa preserves the island's architectural heritage with its sloped ceramic roof, wooden shutters, and exposed stone walls, while offering all modern comforts. The courtyard garden provides a shaded retreat for morning coffee or evening wine under the stars.

You're just a ten-minute stroll from the harbor, where waterfront tavernas serve freshly caught fish and the cobblestone lanes reveal artisan shops, bakeries with local cheese pies, and churches that have stood for centuries. This is Skopelos at its most authentic.
