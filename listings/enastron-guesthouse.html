About This Apartment

A ground floor apartment of a newly built house that has been specifically designed, furnished and decorated to make the most of light and space and also to improve it from a simple place to a spacious and comfortable resting lodge.

Very peaceful, quiet location, no traffic. Ideal for couples, or a family with 1–2 children. Just 5 minutes from the port and the beach, with wonderful views of the surrounding area.

If you need more rooms, it can be combined with our Enastron Guest Room (1 bedroom-bathroom, sleeps 2) that is rented separately.
