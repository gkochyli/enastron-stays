About This Cottage

Set within the beautiful landscapes of Skopelos, this charming cottage accommodates up to 5 guests, making it perfect for couples or small families looking to escape the hustle and bustle. Surrounded by olive, chestnut, and almond trees in peaceful rural settings.

The cottage features modern comforts such as air conditioning, ensuring your stay is both cozy and enjoyable throughout the year. Take a refreshing dip in the private pool, or prepare your favorite meals in the fully equipped kitchen. The terrace offers wonderful views of the surrounding valley and olive groves.

Part of a stunning 15-acre family estate in the Potami valley, between Agnontas and Panormos beaches. Nearby you'll find Limnonari Beach, the Photography Museum, and the Port of Skopelos — all within a short drive. Pet-friendly, so your four-legged companions are welcome too.
