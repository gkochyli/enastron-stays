About This Villa

Nestled among pine trees on the western coast of Skopelos, Kastani Sunset Villa commands jaw-dropping views of the golden sunsets that have made this side of the island legendary. The villa sits just minutes from Kastani Beach — the famous Mamma Mia! filming location.

This spacious four-bedroom retreat features an open-plan living area with floor-to-ceiling windows, a designer kitchen, and an infinity-edge pool that seems to spill into the sea beyond. Each bedroom opens onto a private balcony where you can breathe in the pine-scented air.

Whether you're exploring the island's hidden coves by boat, hiking through ancient olive groves, or simply reading a book by the pool, Kastani Sunset Villa provides the ultimate Mediterranean escape for larger families and groups.
