Discover Skopelos

Known as the greenest island in the Aegean Sea, Skopelos captivates visitors with its dense pine forests, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and whitewashed hillside villages draped in bougainvillea.

Part of the Northern Sporades archipelago, Skopelos gained international fame as the filming location for Mamma Mia! Its charming port town — recognized as a Traditional Settlement of Outstanding Beauty — features narrow cobblestone streets, ceramic-roofed houses, and over 360 churches and chapels.

From the golden sands of Kastani and Panormos beaches to the olive groves and pine-scented hiking trails, every corner of the island invites you to slow down and savor the authentic Greek way of life.
